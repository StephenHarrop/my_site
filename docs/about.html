I'm a Lecturer in the Department of Philosophy at King's College London.

I got my PhD at Yale University. I defended a dissertation entitled Spinoza, Explained in 2022, under the supervision of Michael Della Rocca. Before landing at Yale I studied philosophy, mathematics, and physics at The Johns Hopkins University. Before that, I spent two years at Austin Community College.
