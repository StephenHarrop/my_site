I'm currently a Visiting Assistant Professor at Texas A&M University. In the past I held appointments at Mount Holyoke College and Connecticut College.

I got my PhD at Yale University. I defended a dissertation entitled Spinoza, Explained in 2022, under the supervision of Michael Della Rocca. Before landing at Yale I earned a BA in philosophy and mathematics and an MA in philosophy from The Johns Hopkins University. Before that, I spent two years at Austin Community College.
